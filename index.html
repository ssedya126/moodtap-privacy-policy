Privacy Policy for MoodTap

Last updated: December 2024

Thank you for choosing MoodTap ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MoodTap.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data:

  • Mood Entries: Emotional states, timestamps, and optional notes you voluntarily record
  • App Usage Data: Streak counts, total mood entries, feature usage statistics
  • Purchase Information: Email address (optional, for purchase recovery and backup)
  • Contact Information: Email address (if provided for customer support)
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain mood tracking functionality
  • Store and display your personal mood history and statistics
  • Enable premium feature purchases and restore purchases
  • Process customer support requests and communications
  • Improve app experience and develop new features
  • Maintain streaks, trends, and personal insights

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your mood data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences for immediate access and offline functionality.

Cloud Storage: Data may be securely synchronized with Supabase database for backup and cross-device functionality.

Email Communications: Customer support emails are processed through Brevo's secure email service.

Security: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Store: For app distribution and in-app purchase processing
  • Supabase: For secure cloud data storage and synchronization
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): For email forwarding and customer support communications
  • Google Fonts: For app interface styling
  • Flutter Framework: For app development and functionality

5. Email Communications (Brevo Service)

When you contact us for customer support or provide your email address for purchase recovery, we use Brevo to process and manage these communications. Brevo may collect and process:

  • Email address
  • Contact name (if provided)
  • Message content and metadata
  • Communication history

Brevo's privacy policy can be found at: https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and advertisers.

Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers (like Brevo and Supabase) to perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Email communication management
  • Data storage and synchronization
  • Payment processing

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view all your mood data within the app
  • Export: Use the "Download Data" feature to export your mood history as PDF
  • Deletion: You can delete individual mood entries or uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Correction: You can edit mood notes and entries at any time
  • Email Communications: You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in this Privacy Policy. We will also retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
